Company Overview

Engineering Clean
Energy at Scale

Sterling and Wilson Renewable Energy Limited (SWREL) is a leading provider of end-to-end solar Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) and Operations & Maintenance (O&M) solutions, delivering large-scale renewable energy projects across diverse geographies and environments.

We are one of

India’s top solar EPC players

The world’s premier EPC solutions providers

The leading global solar O&M players

Our expertise spans the entire project lifecycle, from design and detailed engineering to procurement, construction, installation, commissioning and long-term operations.

We support the development of utility-scale and floating solar power projects, enabling reliable and efficient clean energy generation at scale, through our turnkey EPC and Balance of System (BoS) capabilities. We are also expanding our capabilities in solar-plusstorage solutions and wind/hybrid, helping customers unlock greater flexibility, stability and resilience in their energy systems

We continue to partner with clients worldwide to translate renewable energy ambitions into dependable, high-performing assets, guided by engineering excellence, execution discipline and deep sector expertise.
