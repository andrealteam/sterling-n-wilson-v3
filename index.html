Our Journey

Driving the
Solar Transition

Read More

2011

  • Ventured into the solar EPC business

2013

  • Completed our first turnkey EPC project
  • Started international solar operations

2015

  • Recognised as the largest Indian solar EPC player
  • Constructed the largest single-axis tracker plant in India (60 MWp)

2016

  • Diversified into rooftop solar
  • Started third-party O&M services

2017

  • Demerger of Sterling & Wilson Solar from Sterling & Wilson Group
  • Incorporated a subsidiary in Spain
  • Commenced operations in the USA
  • Bagged the world’s largest single-location solar EPC project in Abu Dhabi

2018

  • Expanded operations in Australia by acquiring a controlling stake in GCO Electrical Pty. Ltd.

2019

  • Listed on BSE and NSE
  • Commissioned our first floating solar project of 450 kWp

2020

  • Ranked 2nd largest O&M player in the world by Wood Mackenzie

2021

  • Expanded our offerings to include Hybrid Energy, Energy Storage and Waste-to-Energy solutions

2022

  • Reliance New Energy acquired a 40% stake in SWREL
  • Awarded one of the largest domestic EPC contracts of 1.6 GW by NTPC

2023

  • Achieved a second consecutive mega order (1,500 MW) from NTPC
  • Raised INR 1,500 crore through the QIP route

2024

  • We closed five marquee project wins totalling over INR 2,400 crore
  • Achieved the highest unexecuted order value in SWREL’s history of more than INR 10,500 crore in October 2024

2025

  • Ventured into wind EPC
  • Our solar O&M portfolio crosses 9 GW

2026

  • Commissioned 4.5 GW AC capacities in FY26
  • Achieved highest annual turnover post IPO

Building the Next Curve

It is a year of clarity, where we at Sterling and Wilson Renewable Energy Limited (SWREL) pause to refine the intent, align our strengths and define the direction we want to pursue with conviction.

As the renewable energy landscape expands in scale and complexity, we are thoughtfully shaping our next horizon. We are strengthening the way we execute, broadening the solutions we bring to our partners and reinforcing the discipline that ensures our progress is sustainable. The choices we make today are guided by long-term belief, so that our foundation grows stronger alongside our ambition.

‘Building the Next Curve’

captures this spirit: a deliberate step forward, preparing ourselves for a new, more enduring chapter of growth.
